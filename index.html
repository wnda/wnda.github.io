Intro

I’m Adam, a freelance web developer based in Leeds

I build websites and web apps that are fast, secure, accessible and stylish.

I specialise in full-stack JavaScript, but I’m also a dab hand with ASP.NET (C# or VB) and SQL.

My other acronyms include HTML5, XHTML, AMP-HTML, CSS3, SCSS, JSON & Classic ASP. I was also once rather familiar with Perl.

I’m interested in building progressive web apps, giving the web the silky animation and offline functionality of native apps.

I also consult, and sometimes write about front-end development, among other topics.
